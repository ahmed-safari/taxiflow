Add your dashboard and UI screenshots here.

2. **trips_by_hour.png** - Bar chart showing trips distribution by hour

- **spark_ui.png** - Spark Master UI showing workers

## How to Take Screenshots

### Metabase Dashboard

1. Open http://localhost:3000
2. Navigate to your dashboard
3. Use browser screenshot or press `Cmd+Shift+4` (macOS)

### Airflow DAG

1. Open http://localhost:8081
2. Click on the `nyc_taxi_etl` DAG
3. Switch to "Graph" view
4. Take screenshot

### MinIO

1. Open http://localhost:9001
2. Navigate to the `datalake` bucket
3. Take screenshot showing the data files
